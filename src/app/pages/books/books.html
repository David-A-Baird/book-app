Book 1: From The Ashes

When a bomb falls upon a cluster of towns, a few of the survivors are thrown from the cities. They find themselves in a battle for power and survival, with magic to aid them. They have many skills among them, and meet others along the way who join them. But they are fighting against another faction, including some of their own friends. Who will prevail? Who will fall? And what will they discover about their world as they travel?
